# SleepyStory — Help

**A bedtime story generator demo—focused on delightful UX and safe defaults.**

## What this is
SleepyStory is a creative app demo that generates short stories designed for kids or wind‑down reading. In portfolio terms it demonstrates: friendly UX, safe content boundaries, and a clean “generate → edit → save” flow.

## How to use it
Choose the story settings (character, theme, age range, length) and generate. If illustrations are available, generate them per page or per scene. Use the regenerate option to iterate without losing your chosen settings.

If you’re testing on mobile: try full‑screen reading mode for the intended experience.

## Tips
A good creative generator should:
- keep tone consistent across regenerations
- avoid jarring content for the chosen age range
- keep controls simple (parents should understand it in 10 seconds)

Try changing one variable at a time and see if the output changes in the expected direction.

## Troubleshooting
- **Story is too long**: reduce length setting.
- **Images don’t load**: some builds use placeholders or require a provider; DEMO mode may show static sample art.
- **Text looks cramped**: increase font size or switch to landscape on tablets.

## Privacy + data
In DEMO mode, the story lives in your browser. If you later add sharing, make it opt‑in and explicit.
