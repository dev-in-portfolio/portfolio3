# UBR — Ultimate Broker Routing — Complete Build

## What this is
UBR is a multi-day, multi-stop route planning and prioritization system for field work. It optimizes across geography, lead density, revisit logic, and practical scheduling constraints—beyond single-trip navigation.

## Why this exists
Real field workflows aren’t “shortest path.” They’re prioritization under uncertainty (changing priorities, interruptions, time windows). UBR exists to model that reality and make tradeoffs visible.

## What this proves
- Constraint modeling and practical optimization heuristics
- Spatial reasoning and planning UX
- Designing tools for in-motion/mobile workflows
- Transparent prioritization logic (why this stop first)
- Product thinking anchored in real operations
## One‑liner
**Multi‑day route optimization for brokers** — turn a messy stop list into a realistic schedule you’ll actually follow.

## What it demonstrates
- Constraint-driven planning (days, max stops, time windows)
- Practical UX around route choice (scoreboard, compare, iterate)
- Real-world “field workflow” thinking (save, export, resume)
